# Privacy Policy for Kiado

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

## Introduction

Kiado ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use,
disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

- Name and email address
- Profile information (profile picture, bio)
- Contact information (phone number)
- User type (landlord or tenant)

### Property Information

- Property details (address, description, pricing)
- Property photos
- Availability dates

### Usage Data

- Device information
- Log data and analytics
- Location data (when you grant permission)

### Payment Information

- Payment processing is handled by Stripe
- We do not store your full credit card details

### Communication Data

- Messages between users
- Booking requests and responses

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain our service
- Process bookings and payments
- Facilitate communication between landlords and tenants
- Send notifications about bookings and messages
- Improve our service and user experience
- Prevent fraud and ensure security

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

- **Service Providers**: Supabase (database), Stripe (payments), OpenCage (geocoding)
- **Other Users**: Profile information visible to other users as necessary for the service
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

- Encrypted data storage
- Secure authentication via Supabase
- HTTPS for all data transmission
- Regular security updates

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of certain data collection
- Export your data

## Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion at
any time.

## Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
